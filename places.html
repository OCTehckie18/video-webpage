Meet the Seven Sisters and One Brother!

The Eight States of Northeast India

Arunachal Pradesh

Land of the Dawn-lit Mountains, home to Tawang Monastery and stunning valleys.

 Assam

Known for Kaziranga National Park, tea gardens, and the mighty Brahmaputra.

 Manipur

Famous for Loktak Lake, floating islands, and vibrant culture.

Meghalaya

Abode of Clouds, home to Living Root Bridges and Nohkalikai Falls.

 Mizoram

Land of rolling hills, bamboo forests, and vibrant festivals.

Nagaland

Known for its tribal culture, Hornbill Festival, and scenic villages.

 Tripura

Famous for Neermahal Palace, temples, and lush green landscapes.

 Sikkim

The only brother, known for Gangtok, Kanchenjunga, and monasteries.
