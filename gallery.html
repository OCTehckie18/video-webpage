Must-Visit Natural Destinations

Living Root Bridges, Meghalaya

Marvel at centuries-old bridges grown from living roots in the lush rainforests of Cherrapunji.

Home to the one-horned rhinoceros and a UNESCO World Heritage Site teeming with wildlife.
Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh

A tranquil valley known for its pine-clad hills, rice fields, and the vibrant Apatani culture.
